Bio

Hi! I'm Yomi, a VFx Supervisor at SunnyBoy Entertainment, for Virtual Reality, and cinematic projects. I received my Masters in Visualization(Computer Graphics) at the Department of Visualization, Texas A&M University. My main interests and area of specialty are in layout, texturing/shading, lighting & compositing. I am very passionate about creating interesting images that communicates a story.
In my spare time, I indulge in professional photography one of my most treasured talents that help me grow daily as an artist. Do enjoy my work!

My Thesis

My thesis was focused on exploring how one could adapt cinematic and theatrical lighting to direct viewer attention in VR story scenarios. I created illustrative examples demonstrating how these techniques can be used in Unreal Engine 4.
My committee chair was Dr. Fred Parke, at the Department of Visualization, Texas A&M University.
